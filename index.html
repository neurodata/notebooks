--- layout: home search_exclude: true image: images/logo.png --- This site is a place to host Jupyter Notebooks for the [Neurodata](https://neurodata.io/) lab at Johns Hopkins University. For more information on the lab, visit our [about page](https://neurodata.io/about/), or our [Github](https://github.com/neurodata) to see other projects and open source software. If you are a Neurodata member with write access and would like to add a notebook, check out the [Github repo](https://github.com/neurodata/notebooks) that builds this site. Simply upload your notebook to the [_notebooks](https://github.com/neurodata/notebooks/tree/master/_notebooks) folder, following all of the instructions there. # Posts
